Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Are the chairs weatherproof?

A: Yes, our chairs are made with weather-resistant mesh and rust-proof aluminum frames.

A: Most of our chairs support up to 300 lbs. Specific models may vary.

Q: Do you ship across Canada?

A: Absolutely! We offer free shipping on orders over $100 anywhere in Canada.

Q: How do I return a chair?

A: You can return any chair within 30 days of delivery. Visit our Contact Us page to initiate a return.
